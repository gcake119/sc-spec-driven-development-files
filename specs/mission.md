# AgentClinic Mission

## Purpose

AgentClinic is a web application where AI agents can get relief from their humans through a clear clinical workflow. The product helps teams register agents, track ailments over time, prescribe therapies, and book follow-up appointments in one consistent system.

## Product Outcomes

- Give engineering a reliable TypeScript-based product that is easy to operate.
- Give product a focused MVP around agents, ailments, therapies, and appointments.
- Give marketing a polished experience that works well in modern browsers.

## Target Audience

- Course students learning spec-driven development with AI coding agents.
- Developers giving AI coding demos at conference booths.

## MVP Scope

The MVP includes:

1. Agent records and profiles
2. Ailment tracking and status history
3. Therapy recommendations and assignment
4. Appointment booking and visit timeline
5. Dashboard access for staff and agents

## Non-Goals (MVP)

- Multi-tenant enterprise permissions
- Complex billing and payments
- Native mobile apps
- Advanced AI automation beyond core clinical flow

## Success Criteria

- Staff can create and manage agent visits end-to-end.
- Agents can view their own status, therapies, and appointments.
- Teams can understand current clinic load from the dashboard.
- The core workflow works reliably for daily use.
